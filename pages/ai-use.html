Use of Generative AI (ChatGPT)

During this project, I used generative AI (ChatGPT) as a supportive learning tool to help improve my understanding of web development concepts and to debug small issues in my code.

How I Used AI

  • To get suggestions for color combinations that meet accessibility (WCAG/WAVE) contrast standards.
  • To help troubleshoot broken navigation links in my navbar between pages.
  • To clarify CSS layout and responsiveness issues when elements were not aligning correctly.

Important Note

All design decisions, structure, and final coding were completed by me. AI was only used as a guide for learning, debugging, and improving accessibility and usability—not to generate the full project.
