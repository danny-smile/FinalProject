Ethical Issues in Web Development

As a web developer, I am aware of several ethical issues that can arise during the development process. These include respecting user privacy, ensuring accessibility for all users, avoiding plagiarism by properly citing sources, and creating honest and transparent content. It is important to consider these ethical concerns to build trust with users and create a positive online experience.

Key Ethical Concerns

  • Copyright and proper use of images and content
  • Privacy and protection of user data
  • Accessibility for users with disabilities
  • Avoiding misleading or harmful design practices
